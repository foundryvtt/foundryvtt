Foundry Gaming LLC is Hiring!

Foundry Virtual Tabletop and its community are growing in exciting and dynamic ways. We have a bold vision for the future of Foundry VTT and need to scale up in order to accomplish our goals for 2021 and beyond. We are looking to hire several talented and creative problem solvers who can help make that vision a reality. Current opportunities to join our team are listed below.

To apply please send us an email:

  • To: admin@foundryvtt.com
  • Subject: Employment Opportunities
  • Body: Please provide a brief written introduction and any community contact information that you wish to share
  • Attachments: Your current resume

If we choose to move forward with your application, we will reply by email to schedule an interview.

Foundry Gaming LLC is committed to offering equal employment opportunity to all qualified candidates in pursuit of a diverse and multi-talented team.

Software Developer

As a Software Developer with Foundry Gaming LLC you will work directly with the lead developer to improve the core Foundry Virtual Tabletop software by collecting community feedback, cultivating the backlog of potential enhancements, designing new features, implementing testing procedures. You will contribute to the ongoing health and quality of the Foundry Virtual Tabletop community by providing quality technical documentation and interfacing with community developers to share best practices and expertise. You will engage with customers directly to respond to feedback and support requests as we work together to improve the Foundry Virtual Tabletop product.

This role is designed as a full-time position.

Required Qualifications

  • 2+ years professional experience in a software development field.
  • Experience with web application development, testing, and deployment including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  • A strong understanding of computer science fundamentals including algorithms, data structures, and object-oriented design.
  • Expertise in writing clearly documented, logical, and maintainable code.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and a willingness to translate ambiguous objectives into technical design.
  • Scheduling flexibility to regularly interact with the Foundry team during US daytime hours.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working with WebGL or other low-level rendering languages developing custom graphical shaders.
  • Experience working with Python and Django.
  • Familiarity with a variety of role-playing games and their mechanical systems.
  • Experience developing a game system or module using the Foundry Virtual Tabletop API.
  • Ability to create compelling media through graphic design, illustration, audio engineering, video creation, or live streaming.
  • Enthusiasm for community engagement and advocacy.
