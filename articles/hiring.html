Backend Developer

We are looking for a software engineer with a focus and expertise on backend development to join our team and help us build a next-generation service and platform for our website and business.

As a Backend Developer with the team, you will work directly with the Lead Developer of the software to create, grow, and maintain a web platform. You will be responsible for identifying and scoping product requirements, cultivating a backlog of enhancements, designing new features, implementing testing procedures, and managing deployments. You will contribute to the ongoing health and quality of the platform by providing technical documentation, interfacing with end users, and responding to feedback and support requests related to the product.

This role is designed as a full-time remote work position.

Required Qualifications

  • 2+ years professional experience in a software development field.
  • Expertise with the development, deployment, and testing of backend web architecture.
  • Expertise in writing logical, maintainable, efficient, and clearly documented code.
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and capability to translate ambiguous objectives into a technical design.
  • Scheduling flexibility to routinely interact with our team during US daytime work hours.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience working with Python in the context of backend development.
  • Experience developing using the Django framework.
  • Experience with e-commerce workflows for checkout, payment processing, and order fulfillment.
  • Experience designing cloud architecture and working with cloud services using AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, or other providers.
  • Capabilities with full-stack development to designing front-end user experiences.
  • Familiarity with tabletop role-playing games and the Foundry Virtual Tabletop software.
  • Experience developing modules or systems for Foundry Virtual Tabletop.

To apply please email admin@foundryvtt.com with the subject Backend Developer Application, a brief introduction which aligns your background with the above qualifications, a current résumé, and any key questions you have for us. We will review applications and respond to you promptly with any next steps.

Environment Artist

We are looking for a talented environment artist to work with the content creation side of our business creating immersive gameplay experiences for tabletop role-playing games.

Your artwork will guide and inspire our team to create an unprecedented product in the digital role-playing industry. Our team is building upon the foundation of A House Divided and The Demon Queen Awakens to create something exceptional. This will be a long-term position responsible for creating numerous pieces of high-quality environment artwork over the course of an 18-24 month period.

As an Environmental Artist with our team, you would work closely with the project's Creative Director and Art Director to refine and influence our creative vision, inspire the development of narrative and level design, and incorporate your work to be featured prominently within the final product.

We are open to framing this role as either a full-time remote work position or freelance contract depending on candidate preferences and availability.

Required Qualifications

  • Experience creating professional quality original environmental illustrations with an emphasis on portraying the scale, vibrancy, imagination, and grandeur of fantasy locations.
  • A portfolio of work which showcases quality and breadth of work.
  • Willingness to collaborate with a team of peers to iterate on concepts leading towards final illustrations.
  • Scheduling flexibility to routinely interact with our team during US daytime work hours.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Experience creating artwork assets for video games or web applications which require certain technical specifications for animation, composition, layers, texture formats, or other specialized requirements.
  • Creativity to draw imaginative fantasy locations which extend beyond traditional medieval depictions.
  • Ability to assist the team with other artwork-related tasks like portraiture or graphical illustration.
  • Familiarity with tabletop role-playing games and the Foundry Virtual Tabletop software.

To apply please email admin@foundryvtt.com with the subject Environment Artist Application, a brief introduction which aligns your background with the above qualifications, links to your portfolio of work, a current résumé, and any key questions you have for us. We will review applications and respond to you promptly with any next steps.

Game Writer

We are looking for a talented writer with a focus on game design and tabletop role-playing games to work with our content creation team to create an innovative game experience for Foundry Virtual Tabletop.

Our team is building an entirely new product following the example set by A House Divided and The Demon Queen Awakens, but with significantly expanded scope. This will be a long-term position responsible for writing (English language) significant portions of the game setting, lore, narrative, events, characters, quests, and more over the course of an 18-24 month period.

As a writer with the team, you will work closely with the project's Creative Director, artists, game designers, and fellow writers to craft an immersive and original story in a captivating novel setting for fantasy role-playing.

We are open to framing this role as either a full-time remote work position or freelance contract depending on candidate preferences and availability.

Required Qualifications

  • Professional experience creating and writing for published products in the video games or tabletop role-playing games industry.
  • Familiarity with tabletop role-playing games and their game design, mechanics, gameplay loops, and storytelling tools.
  • Attention to detail and world-building to craft a game setting which is immersive, coherent, and authentic.
  • Scheduling flexibility to routinely interact with our team during US daytime work hours.

Preferred Qualifications

  • Writing credits for published products in the tabletop role-playing games industry, specifically.
  • Experience with game design for a role-playing game system.
  • Experience creating non-linear narratives with branching pathways of choice and consequence.
  • Ability to write locations, characters, cultures, and storylines which draw inspiration from sources outside of traditional medieval fantasy norms.
  • Familiarity with the Foundry Virtual Tabletop software.
