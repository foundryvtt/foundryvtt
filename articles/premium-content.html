Overview

While an overwhelming majority of the content available for Foundry VTT is free for all if its users, the content that can be offered for free is often limited by licensing issues. Premium Content allows you to purchase assets, adventures, game content and book materials already configured for use in Foundry VTT.

Foundry Virtual Tabletop offers access to a variety of premium content thanks to an ever-growing number of awesome publishers and content creatorswho are bringing their games and content to our users. Buying premium content for Foundry VTT places emphasis on keeping the content you've purchased in your hands, so you can use it the way you want to. Our approach to premium content allows publishers to sell products built for use in Foundry VTT directly to their customer base, while allowing their users to install directly from within our software for maximum ease of use.

Every premium content package offered by Foundry Virtual Tabletop can be found on the Premium Content packages list. Each package is required to provide a link to the publisher's store where you may purchase your product key for that package. Foundry VTT does not operate a storefront or marketplace, and does not handle the financial transaction of purchasing the Premium Content, that is wholly administrated by the publisher or creator.

Activation and Installation

Once you have purchased your Premium Content from the publisher of your choosing, you should receive an email containing your activation key and the processes needed to activate it. Please note that activating the content key binds it to your Foundry VTT account, and once activated it cannot be claimed by another user. It is important to make sure you are activating the content key on the correct account.

To activate a key:

  1. Login to the Foundry VTT Website
  2. Click your username in the upper right corner to access your profile page
  3. Click the "Premium Content" Button in the lower left
  4. Copy and paste your content key into the "Content Key" field
  5. Click the "Activate Content" button

Once activated the Content Key is added to your Foundry VTT account and no further steps are required. Your premium content will appear in your "Unlocked Premium Content" list, showcasing what content you have permission to access via the Premium Content section of the package installer in Foundry Virtual Tabletop.

Installing Premium Content

Whether it is a World, a Game System, or an Add-on Module, installation of your premium content uses Foundry VTT's Package Installer. If you aren't certain which category your newly purchased content falls into, check the content's page on the Foundry VTT homepage to identify it (though it is usually an Add-on Module.)

To install premium content:

  1. From the Setup menu within Foundry VTT, select the appropriate tab for your content: Game Worlds, Game Systems, or Add-on Modules.
  2. On this tab, click the install button in the bottome left corner.
  3. In the installation menu that appears, filter the view by clicking the "Premium Content" category on the left hand side.
  4. Locate your Premium Content within the list and click "Install" on the right hand side of each item you own.

If your content displays "Not Owned" instead of an installation button please verify that you have activated the content for your account.
